HOW TO USE

  1. Click ENABLE MIC and allow browser permissions.
  2. Use the Search bar to find a karaoke track on YouTube.
  3. Click a result to add it to the UP NEXT queue.
  4. Sing along. Points are awarded while your mic is active during the track.
  5. When the song ends, enter your name to save your score to the leaderboard.
